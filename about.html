AKO BICOL PARTY LIST

Serving the Bicol Region since 2009 through dedicated representation and sustainable development initiatives

Our Mission

AKO BICOL Party List is committed to uplifting the lives of Bicolanos through effective legislation, sustainable development programs, and responsive governance. We work tirelessly to address the unique challenges faced by the Bicol Region and its people.

Our Journey

2009

Foundation

AKO BICOL Party List was established to represent the interests of the Bicol Region.

2010

First Congressional Term

Successfully won seats in Congress and began implementing programs for Bicolanos.

2013-2016

Expanded Programs

Launched comprehensive disaster preparedness and education initiatives.

2016-2019

Infrastructure Development

Focused on improving regional infrastructure and connectivity.

2019-Present

Continued Service

Expanding reach and impact through various development programs.
